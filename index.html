The Cornell Bathroom Finder

A public filtering site for Cornell's on-campus bathrooms

Welcome to The Cornell Bathroom Finder!

Your go-to resource for locating restrooms across Cornell's campus. Whether you're searching for a bathroom with specific amenities, need to find accessible options, or just want to know where the nearest restroom is, our tool is here to help.

Key Features

  • Searchable Database: Easily search for bathrooms by building, floor, gender, and other important criteria.
  • Real-time Filters: Quickly filter restrooms by gender, accessibility features, number of stalls, and more.
  • Accessible Information: Get detailed information about each bathroom, including whether itâ€™s ADA accessible, has changing tables, menstrual products, and more.

Why Use The Cornell Bathroom Finder?

Navigating large campuses can be difficult, especially when you're in a rush or need specific amenities. Our tool helps you find restrooms that best suit your needs. Whether you're looking for a single-occupancy bathroom or a gender-neutral space, this platform allows you to make more informed choices based on real-time data.

How It Works

  1. Select Filters: Use the search bar and filter options to narrow down your choices.
  2. View Results: Our table displays restrooms across campus with all relevant details, such as the building name, floor, gender options, and amenities.
  3. Get Directions: Plan your route based on the available locations and find the bathroom that suits your needs.
